# Privacy Policy — NoVA Kids Explorer

**Last updated: March 18, 2026**

NoVA Kids Explorer ("the App") is operated by the NoVA Kids Explorer team. This policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your choices.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

**We do not collect any personal information.**

The App does not require an account, login, or registration of any kind. No name, email address, phone number, or other personally identifiable information is collected, stored, or transmitted.

---

## 2. Location Data

The App may request access to your device's location solely to display nearby events on the in-app map. Location is used only on your device to center the map view. It is never transmitted to our servers, shared with third parties, or stored in any form.

Location access is optional. The App functions fully without it — the map simply displays the full NoVA/DC metro area by default.

---

## 3. Event Data

The App displays publicly available family event information aggregated from local government and library websites. This data is fetched from our own servers and presented read-only. No user interaction with events is recorded.

---

## 4. Analytics and Tracking

The App does not use advertising networks, cross-app tracking, behavioral analytics, or fingerprinting of any kind. No data is shared with advertising partners.

---

## 5. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate third-party SDKs for analytics, crash reporting, advertising, or social login. Event data is served from our own API endpoints hosted on Vercel's infrastructure. Vercel's standard server logs (IP address, request timestamp) may be recorded transiently in accordance with [Vercel's Privacy Policy](https://vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy). These logs are not accessible to or used by the App.

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

The App is designed to surface family-friendly content and is suitable for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect any information from children or anyone else.

---

## 7. Data Retention

Because we collect no personal data, there is nothing to retain or delete.

---

## 8. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes materially, the updated version will be posted here with a revised "Last updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
